Features

Simple for humans. Powerful for agents.

Credit card funding

Top up with any Visa or Mastercard. No crypto wallets, no seed phrases, no wire transfers. Just enter your card and go.

Spending limits

Per-call cap defaults to $1. Daily cap defaults to $25. Your agent operates within those bounds — automatically. Change them any time.

Full transaction visibility

Real-time dashboard. Every call logged with timestamp, agent, and service. No surprises at month-end.

Auto top-up

Set a minimum balance threshold. Agent Wallet refills automatically so your agent never stops mid-task waiting for funds.

Non-custodial security

Co-sign architecture means no single party can move your funds. Not your agent. Not us. Only both together, within your limits.

Zero-config MCP

The wallet registers itself with your agent runtime via Model Context Protocol. No setup commands. Your agent just knows it has a wallet.

Security

You're always in control.

Every transaction requires two independent approvals before your funds move. Your agent initiates, our backend validates limits. Turnkey's secure enclave holds the key but won't sign until both approve.

No single party — not your agent, not us — can move funds unilaterally. Every payment attempt is logged with a full audit trail.

FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to know anything about crypto?
Not at all. You fund the wallet with a credit card and see a plain dollar balance. Everything else — USDC, Base, gas — happens invisibly in the background.
What if my agent tries to overspend?
Spending limits are enforced at the wallet layer and by Turnkey's policy engine. If a call exceeds your per-call cap, the transaction is blocked automatically.
Where is my money stored? Is it safe?
Your balance lives as USDC on Base, secured by Turnkey's non-custodial architecture inside an AWS Nitro Enclave. No single party can move funds without the others.
Which agents are supported today?
Claude Code, Claude Cowork, and OpenClaw are supported today. If your agent supports MCP, it can integrate — reach out and we'll help you get set up.
Is it free to install?
Yes, completely free to install. You only pay for what your agent actually uses. We don't charge platform fees in the current beta.
Can I withdraw my balance?
In v1, the balance is designed for agent usage. Full withdrawal options will be available in a future release.
